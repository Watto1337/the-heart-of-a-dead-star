Evening Horizon The Evening Horizon is the part of the world that is eclipsed just after nightfall. It spans two semi-circles diagonally opposite each other. Morning Horizon The Morning Horizon is the similar part of the world that is eclipsed shortly before morning. To describe directions on the surface of Arnsdale, North and South refer to the direction tangent to the surface that leads towards the north or south pole, respectively. Darth is the direction towards the dark side of the planet, and Lith is the direction towards the light side of the planet.
